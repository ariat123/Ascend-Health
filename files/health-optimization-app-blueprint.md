## THE GAMIFIED STRUCTURE

### Phase 1: Initial Assessment (The Big Quiz)

**10-15 minutes, gamified like a personality test:**

**Section 1: Current State**
- Energy level (1-10)
- Sleep quality
- Gut issues?
- Brain fog?
- Skin problems?
- Allergies?

**Section 2: Goals**
- What do you want most? (Energy, sleep, gut health, focus, etc.)
- Rank top 3

**Section 3: Current Habits**
- Diet (SAD, clean eating, specific diet?)
- Exercise?
- Sleep routine?
- Morning/evening routines?
- Supplements? (none, basic, advanced)

**Section 4: Constraints**
- Budget for supplements (none, $50/mo, $100+/mo)
- Time available (15min/day, 30min, 1hr)
- Access to equipment (sauna, cold plunge, red light?)

**Output: Your Optimization Score (0-100) + Personalized 90-Day Protocol**

---

### Phase 2: Your 90-Day Protocol (AI Generated)

**Week 1-2: Foundation**
- Morning routine: Get 10min morning sunlight within 1hr of waking
- Diet: Remove seed oils, focus on whole foods
- Evening: No blue light 2hrs before bed
- Supplements: Start with basics (magnesium, vitamin D)

**Week 3-4: Gut Reset**
- Eliminate gluten/dairy for 2 weeks (trial)
- Add: Digestive enzymes
- Track symptoms daily

**Week 5-8: Optimization**
- Add: Cold exposure (showers) or sauna if available
- Optimize supplement timing
- Track energy, sleep, gut

**Week 9-12: Fine-Tuning**
- AI analyzes your progress
- Adjusts protocol based on what's working
- Suggests advanced protocols

**The Beauty:** It's STRUCTURED. Not "here's 100 things to do." It's "do THIS for 2 weeks, THEN this."
